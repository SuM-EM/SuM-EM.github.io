Research Tools

Overleaf is an online collaborative writing platform based on LaTeX for producing high-quality scientific documents. It allows multiple users to work on the same document simultaneously. Overleaf also integrates with popular reference management tools, such as Zotero and Mendeley manage their references.  Visit

Wolfram Alpha is a computational engine to answer factual queries and provide computed knowledge from various fields of knowledge, including mathematics, physics, engineering. It not only computes answers to questions but also the steps taken to arrive at the solution. Wolfram Alpha can also generate visualizations, and provide insights on a wide range of topics.  Visit
